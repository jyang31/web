Multiplication

Multiplication is one of the four operations. If you arrange the four operations alphabetically, it is the third operation.

Above is the symbol that is used to multiply things. It has two lines, and they cross in the middle. They are diagonal.

Another thing that is diagonal is the hypotenuse of a right triangle. See a right triangle below.

To multiply numbers you multiply them. 2*2=4. This is important too. Multiplication is the basis for a lot of things. For example, if you multiply something by itself a number of times, that is exponentation.

Fun fact: did you know that "multiplication" has the letter "I" three times in it? "I" sounds a lot like "eye", doesn't it? Have a picture of something with three eyes.
