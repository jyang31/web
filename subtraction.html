Subtraction

Subtraction is one of the four operations. If you arrange the four operations alphabetically, subtraction comes last.

Above is the symbol that is used to subtract things. It has one line that is horizontal. It is horizontal.This is unique, as all the other symbols use more than just one line! Subtraction is cool!

To subtract something means to take stuff away from it. There are many other symbols that are similar to the subtraction symbol. See one below.

The strikethrough symbol, used in text, also means to take text away from a document! Isn't that cool?

To subtract numbers you subtract them. 3-1=2. This is also important. Without subtraction, there would be no way to express taking things away.

Fun fact: did you know that "subtraction" has the word "action" in it. Maybe this is because when you subtract something, you are performing the action of subtraction. Wow.

Above is an image of an ACTION.

Subtraction is very different from other operations. While addition and multiplication grows numbers, subtraction decreases numbers.
